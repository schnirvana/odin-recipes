Sandwich

Description

This sandwich is going to be a spectacular display of layers.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Marinara
  • Ham
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients on top of each other
  2. Roll a 6 sided die 10 times
  3. Use randomness as a substitute for an actual meal
